About Us

Welcome to our Apartment Rent Company
  • About Company
  • Our Services

Colombo Residencies has taken the lead to provide affordable hotel-style luxury accommodation options in Colombo, the capital city of Sri Lanka. That is why we offer the very best solutions with our fully furnished luxury service apartments in Colombo. Offering the very best luxuries of city living, Colombo is a prime location to reside in as it is surrounded by a number of key locations.
Each of these locations infuses their own unique flavour and character to make the entire city one of the most happening cities in the world. Crescat Residencies is the true embodiment of Colombo city living so we invite you to join with us to enjoy the wonders of Colombo city living.

Luxury Apartments for Short Let. Studio, 1, 2, and 3 Room apartments for Short Let in Colombo 3, Daily, Weekly and Monthly. All the apartments are Fully Furnished including TV, Washing Machine, Fridge, Ironing, and Microwave.
Fully Equipped Kitchen including Cutlers and Crockeries. Linen also provided. Amenities include International Channels, Wi-Fi, Hot Water, Lift, 24 hours Security and Free Parking, Gym and Pool on selected apartments.
