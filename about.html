Crescat Investments (Pvt) Ltd also known as CIPL is one of the leading property management company in Sri Lanka. The company was under East West Properties PLC (EWP). As you may already know, East West Properties PLC is a leading conglomerate that boasts of a proud history dating back to 1975. The business has now greatly expanded to many areas of operation including Destination Management, Financial Services and Media. As a well-established company, EWP currently manages an impressive investment portfolio in Sri Lanka and Singapore. The value of the value of the portfolio amounts to over LKR 300 million in Sri Lanka and Singapore.
